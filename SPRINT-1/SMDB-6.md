# Text editors

## Vi/Vim
- **Undo**: `u`
- **Cut/Copy**: `x` `y`
- **Insert**: `i`
- **Save**: `:w`
- **Quit**: `:q`
- **Force save**: `:w!`
- **Force quit**: `:q!`
- **Jump to line**: `:<line_number>`
- **Search word**: `/<word>`

## Nano
- **Undo**: `Alt-u`
- **Cut/Copy**: `Ctrl-k` `Alt-6`
- **Save**: `Ctrl-o`
- **Quit**: `Ctrl-x`
- **Force save**: `Ctrl-o Y`
- **Force quit**: `Ctrl-x Y`
- **Jump to line**: `Ctrl+_ <line_number>`
- **Search word**: `Ctrl-w <word>`
